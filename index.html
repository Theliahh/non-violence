    Welcome to VK Kool's Nonviolence and Aggression Test.

    This exam was created by Dr. Vinod K. Kool as a means to identify one's ability to act in nonviolent ways.

    The Test

    The Nonviolence Test, or NVT, is 65 questions, each with 2 answers, A and B. Each question will help build the final answer of the user's non-violence score. Each nonviolent answer increases your score, to a maximum of 36 points.

    Test Results

    Test results are totally free and are identical to those one would receive if a psychologist physically administered the exam. The exam here is identical to the original and was made to replace physical distribution of the exam.
    The studies behind the test were completed by Dr Kool when developing the exam, as outlined here.

    Authors and Contributors
